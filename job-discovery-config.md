- Relevant to one of the 7 target function areas (Revenue, Product, Growth, Operations, Marketing, Ecommerce, CRM)
- Seniority level: C-Level/Executive, VP, or Director (Head level acceptable if growth-focused)
- Location matches one of the tiers above
- **Language is English, French, or Spanish** (NOT Polish-only or other languages)
- **NOT located in USA** (unless explicitly remote with statement "remote, no US territory required")

### Nice-to-Have
- B2B SaaS or tech background
- Growth-stage companies (Series B+)
- Remote-friendly culture
- Multi-cultural/international team environment

### Automatic Exclusions
- Fully on-site outside of Warsaw/Poland without remote option
- Roles below Director level
- Irrelevant industries (hospitality, retail non-tech, academia unless executive)
- **Language requirement: Polish-only, German-only, or languages you don't speak**
- Pure functional roles (Marketing Coordinator, Sales Rep, etc.) without strategic leadership scope
- **Based in USA** (unless explicitly stated "Remote worldwide, no US presence required")

## Output Requirements (Dashboard Display)

For each job displayed, include:

1. **Job Link** — Direct link to job posting (company website or job board)
2. **Salary Range** — If available, display as: EUR/GBP/USD + range (e.g., "€120-150K" or "Not specified")
3. **Location Details** — When not in Poland:
   - Specify: Remote / Hybrid / On-site
   - For hybrid: City + "Hybrid" (e.g., "Berlin, Germany - Hybrid")
   - For remote: "Remote (EU)" or "Remote (Worldwide)" as applicable
4. **Date Posted** — When posting date available (e.g., "Posted: 2 days ago" or "Posted: May 9, 2026")
